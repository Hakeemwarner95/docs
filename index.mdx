LandScout is a land intelligence API that takes any US property address and analysis mode, then returns a structured report with category scores, confidence ratings, source citations, and a downloadable PDF. Whether you're screening properties for off-grid living, rural construction, recreational use, or investment potential, LandScout gives you the data in seconds — without building your own data pipeline.

## How LandScout works

LandScout pulls data from NREL, USGS, FEMA, and county regulation sources, then uses AI to synthesize a structured report. Every report includes:

- **Scores** — an overall score (0–100) plus per-category sub-scores
- **Confidence ratings** — high, medium, or low per category
- **Citations** — structured source list with URLs and access dates
- **PDF** — a downloadable report with a 60-minute signed URL

Report generation is asynchronous and typically completes in 60–120 seconds. You receive results via webhook or by polling the report endpoint.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create your account at [landscout.io/signup](https://landscout.io/signup). Your API keys are shown once immediately after sign-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a mode">
    Choose the analysis mode that matches your use case: `off_grid`, `rural_residential`, `recreational`, or `investment`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a property">
    `POST /v1/analyze` with an address, state, and mode. You receive a `report_id` and a `poll_url` immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive results">
    LandScout delivers the completed report to your webhook endpoint, or you can poll `GET /v1/reports/{id}` until the status is `completed`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
